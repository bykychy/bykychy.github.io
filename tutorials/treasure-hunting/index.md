<div class="info-box tip">
  <strong>Why combine methods?</strong>
  SAR shows landscape behavior, electromagnetic tools reveal conductivity structure, GPR reveals shallow geometry, and XRF reveals composition. Together they form a much stronger evidence stack than any one instrument alone.
