<div class="info-box tip">
  <strong>Why combine methods?</strong>
  SAR shows landscape behavior, electromagnetic tools reveal conductivity structure, GPR reveals shallow geometry, magnetometry detects burned features and iron, XRF reveals composition, and geochemistry traces human activity. Together they form a much stronger evidence stack than any one instrument alone.
</div>
