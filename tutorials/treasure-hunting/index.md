# ⛏️ Treasure Hunting Technologies

Master the science and field techniques behind modern subsurface detection methods. These tutorials cover the physics, mathematics, and practical know-how you need to survey the steppes, mountain valleys, and ancient Silk Road corridors of Central Asia.

<div class="info-box tip">
  <strong>🏔️ Central Asian Context</strong>
  Central Asia — particularly Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, and Tajikistan — sits at the crossroads of ancient civilizations. The Silk Road, Scythian burial mounds (kurgans), and Turkic stone monuments make this region incredibly rich for archaeological prospection using modern technology.
</div>
